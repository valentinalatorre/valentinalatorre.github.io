Welcome!

I am a first year graduate student at Tufts University and I am currently working with my advisor Prof. Anna Sajina, using artificial neural networks in order to derive galaxy parameters for large datasets. I am interested in galaxy formation and evolution, extragalactic astrophysics, and machine learning applications in Astrophysics.

In my spare time I play piano, make fresh pasta, and enjoy exploring Boston area.
